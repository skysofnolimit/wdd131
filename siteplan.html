Cursive Site Plan

Lori Merriam

Overview

Site Name: Cursive has Value

The name explains the message the site is meant to convey.

Purpose

The purpose is to show the value of cursive in the past, present and future. It will also be an aide for those seeking to read the information from historical documents writen in cursive.

Audience and Scenarios

Who should learn cursive?

Are there documents of value worth understanding written in cursive?

Are there benefits to learning to write in cursive?

How do you read the writing on a family members historical document?
